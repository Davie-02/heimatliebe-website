The Institute is currently making inquiries regarding upcoming Goethe examination dates in Tanzania, though we have had new dates published on the Goethe Website as seen in the attached picture. Please note that the other image attached is for the exams costs. 

At present, Tanzania remains the Institute's preferred examination location because management is able to coordinate examination arrangements more effectively and has better access to official examination information.

Once official examination dates and arrangements are confirmed, students and parents will be informed immediately. The institute kindly requests parents who have their children preparing for any upcoming exams to be prepared financially so we can process payments without problems once official dates are confirmed.

Students who wish to sit for Goethe examinations in Zimbabwe or anywhere else are free to do so independently. However, such arrangements must be made entirely at the student's own discretion and expense.

The Institute will not be responsible for travel arrangements, accommodation, examination registration, financial costs, or any other matters relating to examinations undertaken independently in Zimbabwe or other centres.
